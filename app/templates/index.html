PraxTrain 2018

We desire to live out a theology that works in our world and culture today. As followers of Jesus, we want our beliefs, our teachings, and our doctrines to be lived out in our day to day lives in tangible and practical ways.

The Six Elements of Practice

PraxTrain was created to help people do this through 6 elements of practice:

  • - Bible Reading
  • - Scripture Memorization
  • - Verse Meditation
  • - Journal Reflection
  • - Intercessory Prayer
  • - Faith Activation

The ADS Track

The ADS track was developed for students in the Antioch Discipleship School and follows the schools reading schedule (about 30min-1hour of reading and reflecting).

The Praxis Track

The Praxis track is for those with busier schedules but desire the daily prompts to be in the word and be living out their faith (15-30 min). You can jump into either track, it's up to you!
